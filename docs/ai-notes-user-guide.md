# AI-Generated Notes - User Guide

## What's New? 🎉

Your study notes just got a major upgrade! Instead of manually writing notes, you now have an AI-powered study companion that creates personalized, comprehensive notes tailored to YOUR learning journey.

## How It Works

### Step 1: Access AI Notes
1. Open any topic in your study path
2. Navigate to the "Notes" tab
3. You'll see an attractive overview of the AI Notes feature

### Step 2: Generate Your Notes
Click the "Generate AI Notes" button to open the AI Notes modal

### Step 3: Customize Your Experience

**Choose Your Language:**
- **English**: Clear, simple language perfect for NEET preparation
- **Hinglish**: Hindi-English mix for better understanding
  - Example: "Cell membrane ek selectively permeable barrier hai jo cell ko protect karta hai"

**Select Your Format:**
- 📚 **Comprehensive**: Full detailed notes with all concepts
- 🧮 **Formula Sheet**: Organized formulas with examples and usage
- ⚡ **Quick Revision**: Ultra-concise points for last-minute prep
- ⚠️ **Common Mistakes**: Pitfalls to avoid and how to fix them

### Step 4: Progressive Learning

Notes are divided into sections that unlock as you progress:

#### Comprehensive Format Sections:
1. ✅ **Foundation Concepts** - Always available
2. 🔒 **Core Theory** - Unlocks at 30% video completion
3. 🔒 **Problem-Solving Techniques** - Unlocks at 60% video completion
4. 🔒 **Advanced Applications** - Unlocks after taking a quiz
5. 🔒 **Exam Strategy & Quick Revision** - Unlocks at 80% mastery

#### Formula Sheet Sections:
1. ✅ **Essential Formulas** - Always available
2. 🔒 **Derived Formulas** - Unlocks at 40% progress
3. 🔒 **Problem-Solving Shortcuts** - Unlocks after quiz
4. 🔒 **Common Formula Mistakes** - Unlocks at 70% mastery

### Step 5: Study and Progress

- **Expand sections** by tapping on them
- **View unlock conditions** for locked sections
- **Track your progress** at the bottom of the modal
- **Share notes** using the share button in the header

## Tips for Best Results

### 1. Start with Foundation
Always read the "Foundation Concepts" section first - it's unlocked by default and builds your base understanding.

### 2. Watch Videos First
Complete videos to unlock more note sections. This ensures you have context before diving deep.

### 3. Take Quizzes
Attempting quizzes unlocks advanced sections and helps personalize your notes based on performance.

### 4. Use Different Formats
- **Before studying**: Comprehensive notes
- **During practice**: Formula sheets
- **Before exams**: Quick revision
- **After mistakes**: Common mistakes guide

### 5. Switch Languages
If a concept is hard to understand in English, try Hinglish - sometimes mixing languages helps!

### 6. Share with Friends
Use the share button to export notes and study together.

## Understanding Progress Indicators

At the bottom of the notes modal, you'll see:

- **Video Progress**: Percentage of videos watched
- **Quiz Attempts**: Number of quizzes taken
- **Mastery Level**: Overall understanding (0-100%)

These metrics determine which sections unlock!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need internet to view notes?
A: You need internet to generate notes initially. Once generated, you can view them offline.

### Q: Can I regenerate notes?
A: Yes! Change language or format, and notes regenerate automatically.

### Q: Why are some sections locked?
A: To encourage progressive learning. Complete videos and quizzes to unlock more content.

### Q: Which language should I choose?
A: Try both! Use English for technical precision, Hinglish for easier understanding.

### Q: How long does generation take?
A: Usually 5-10 seconds. The AI is creating personalized content just for you!

### Q: Can I print the notes?
A: Use the share button to export, then print from your device.

### Q: Will notes change if I retake a quiz?
A: Yes! Notes adapt based on your latest performance and weak areas.

### Q: What if I don't understand something?
A: Try switching to Hinglish, or use the "Common Mistakes" format for clarification.
